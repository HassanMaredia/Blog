About BlogSpace

Welcome to BlogSpace – a modern, minimalist blogging platform built with pure HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.

This platform allows you to:

  • Create and publish blog posts instantly
  • Store blogs in your browser's localStorage
  • Search and filter through your blogs
  • Toggle between light and dark mode
  • Delete posts you no longer need

Your blogs persist even after refreshing the page. This is a fully frontend solution with no backend required.

Happy blogging!
